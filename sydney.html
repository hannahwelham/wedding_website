    After a wintry Bristol wedding, we'll be jumping on a plane to do the whole thing again on Sydney's summery north shore! Celebrations will kick off in the afternoon of Wednesday 30th December with a boat trip to Athol Hall, Bradley's Head, for a blessing and barbie.

    It will be possible to board the ferry at the Commissioner's Steps (Circular Quay, 3pm) or Kirribilli Wharf (3.15pm). A glass or two of champagne will be waiting for you on deck! To get everybody home at the end of the evening, the boat will do the same trip in reverse at 10.30pm.

    Ladies, please just bring thongs or comfortable shoes to ensure that you can make the most of Athol's beach and National Park grounds!
